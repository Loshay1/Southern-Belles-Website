Catering FAQ

We recommend booking at least 5-7 days in advance for most events. For large events (50+ guests), please give us 2 weeks notice when possible. We'll do our best to accommodate last-minute requests — just give us a call at (224) 699-9959.

Our Half Pans serve 7-9 guests and Full Pans serve 12-15 guests, with prices starting at $49.99 per pan. We also offer per-person options like the Big Breakfast ($13.99/person) and Deli Party Platter ($13.99/person). For smaller groups, consider placing a large takeout order through our regular online ordering system.

Yes! We deliver throughout Carpentersville, Algonquin, Elgin, Huntley, Barrington, East and West Dundee, Lake in the Hills, Gilberts, and surrounding Kane and McHenry County communities. Delivery fees vary by distance. Pickup is always available at our restaurant at 152 S. Western Ave.

Absolutely. While we offer preset packages for convenience, we're happy to build a custom menu from our full selection of 80+ items. We also accommodate gluten-free, vegetarian, and vegan dietary needs. Just let us know your preferences when you submit your quote request.

All hot items are packaged in insulated catering containers to keep them at the proper temperature during transport. For large events, we can discuss setup options including chafing dishes and warming trays. We time our cooking to ensure everything arrives fresh.

All catering orders include disposable plates, napkins, utensils, and serving supplies. Condiments (syrup, butter, hot sauce, etc.) are included as appropriate for your menu selections. If you need any additional serving equipment, let us know and we'll do our best to accommodate.
